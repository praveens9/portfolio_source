About Me

Backend Software Engineer with 12+ years of experience

I am a backend engineer specializing in the design and delivery of high-scale logistics and fulfilment systems. Over the past decade, I have navigated the transition of enterprise platforms from legacy .NET monoliths to modern, event-driven Java microservices, ensuring operational stability at every step.

My expertise lies in solving complex domain problems within store fulfilment—optimizing picking algorithms, resource management, and task orchestration. I have been a foundational engineer on multiple core initiatives, including the Fulfilment Platform System (FPS) and the Micro-Fulfilment Centre (MFC) project, where I focused on architectural analysis, vendor integration, and building resilient data flows.

I take pride in my ability to bridge the gap between business requirements and technical implementation, leveraging deep domain context to build software that is both scalable and maintainable.
